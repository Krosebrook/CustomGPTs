# Creative Prompts

This directory contains prompts designed to inspire and assist with creative endeavors.

## 📁 Types of Prompts

- **Writing Assistance**: Story development, character creation, plot generation
- **Art & Design**: Creative briefs, design concepts, artistic inspiration  
- **Content Creation**: Blog ideas, social media content, marketing copy
- **Brainstorming**: Idea generation, creative problem-solving

## 🎯 Best For
- Writers and authors
- Content creators and marketers
- Artists and designers
- Anyone seeking creative inspiration

## 💡 Tips for Use
- Provide context about your project or goals
- Specify your target audience when relevant
- Ask for multiple variations to explore different directions
- Build on initial ideas with follow-up prompts

## 📝 Contributing
When adding creative prompts, ensure they:
- Encourage originality and innovation
- Work across different skill levels
- Include diverse examples and applications
- Provide clear guidance for customization
